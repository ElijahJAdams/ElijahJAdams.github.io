About Me

My name is Eli, I bounced around a general area in Vermont. I’m the youngest of three and was always the loud annoying sibling. I’m a game designer who attends the Center for Technology, Essex and have a passion for games. In 2022 I won the SkillsUSA Interactive Application and Game Development State Competition and attended the national competition in Atlanta, Georgia. I hope to attend college for game design and get a cushy desk job someday. In my spare time I enjoy video games, messing with my car and hanging out with my friends.
